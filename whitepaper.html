STATIC REWARDS, TOKEN BURNS & LIQUIDITY POOL

Static rewards provide top-notch solution for ETHEREUMGOLD. First, the reward amount is conditional upon the volume of the token being traded. Second, the reflect mechanism encourages holders to hang onto their tokens to garner higher kick-backs which are based upon a percentages carried out and dependant upon the total tokens held by the owner.

Moreover, ETHEREUMGOLD aims to implement a burn strategy that is beneficial and rewarding for those engaged for the long term.

ETHEREUMGOLD's Automatic LP have a function that acts as a two-fold beneficial implementation for holders. First, the contract sucks up tokens from sellers and buyers alike, and adds them to the LP creating a solid price floor. Second, the penalty acts as an arbitrage resistant mechanism that secures the volume of ETHGOLD as a reward for the holders. As the token LP increases, the price stability mirrors this function with the benefit of a solid price floor and cushion for holders.

TRADE TAX

ETHEREUMGOLD’s trade transaction is taxed a 10% fee, which is split 3 ways.

6% fee = redistributed to all existing ETHGOLD holders.
2% is sold for liquidity.
2% tokens are automatically paired and added as a liquidity pair on PancakeSwap.
